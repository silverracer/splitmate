About Page

SplitMate was created with the vision of making bill-splitting easier and more efficient for everyone. We understand that managing shared expenses can be a hassle, especially when it comes to calculating who owes what. That's why we developed SplitMate to simplify the process and provide a seamless experience for users. Our team is dedicated to continuously improving the app and adding new features based on user feedback. We believe that SplitMate can help foster better communication and financial transparency among friends, family, and roommates. Thank you for choosing SplitMate as your go-to bill-splitting solution!
